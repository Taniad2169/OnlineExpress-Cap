Express Capital Network Associate is not a lender or a bank. We do not provide loans or make credit decisions. Our service connects businesses like yours with a network of trusted third-party funding providers who offer Merchant Cash Advances (MCAs) and other commercial financing products.

A Merchant Cash Advance (MCA) is a purchase of future receivables, not a loan. It is not subject to the same regulatory requirements as traditional loans and may have different terms and conditions. Please be aware that MCAs may involve higher costs compared to traditional financing options.

All funding offers are subject to the individual provider's underwriting and approval processes. Express Capital Network Associate may receive a referral fee or commission if your business is funded through one of our partners.
